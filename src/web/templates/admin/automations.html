Automations & Triggers

Build rules visually — no JSON to write. Two flavours: Triggers fire instantly on ticket events (created, status changed, comment added, SLA breach, AI insight…). Schedulers fire on a clock (every N hours, at 9am weekdays, after a ticket has been Open for 24h…). Conditions can read anything on the ticket including custom fields, SLA state, AI signals, and form state. Actions can change the ticket, notify people, call AI, hit a webhook, or apply a macro.
