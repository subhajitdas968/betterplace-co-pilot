Business hours

Define when the SLA clock ticks. Each schedule has a timezone, weekly intervals, and a holiday calendar. The default schedule is used by any SLA policy that doesn't pick one explicitly. Customer-specific overrides are set on /admin/sla.
💡 Create as many schedules as you need. One per timezone, one per shift, one for each customer that runs different hours. Then link them on the SLA page (per-policy default) or per-customer (override on /admin/sla → bottom card). Click + New schedule at the top right.
